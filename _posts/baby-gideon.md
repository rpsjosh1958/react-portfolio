They used to call me Baby Gideon. I don't remember the exact year, but I can still see the orphanage walls clearly. Those early days were filled with a bunch of us kids, special days where we'd feast on jollof, and those sweet, teddy bear-shaped vitamin C tablets that felt like a treat.

One day, an older woman came to pick me up, and I remember getting a toy gun at a store before heading home. I actually thought my aunt was my mother at first! She's the first person I ever called 'Mummy'. My name is Josh now, but I know Gideon is still a part of my story. There's more to tell, but that's where it all begins.
